# Companion Leveling

Freed Dvergr are persistent RPG companions, not vanilla star-tier creatures. Each ally has its own **XP and level track**, saved on the creature itself, so it grows with you and keeps that growth across relogs and server restarts.

## Levels 1 → 10

Companions level from **1 to 10** on a rising XP curve — each level costs more than the last, so the early levels come quickly and the later ones are a long-term goal.

| Level → next | XP needed |
|---|---|
| 1 → 2 | 100 |
| 2 → 3 | 250 |
| 3 → 4 | 450 |
| 4 → 5 | 700 |
| 5 → 6 | 1,000 |
| 6 → 7 | 1,400 |
| 7 → 8 | 1,900 |
| 8 → 9 | 2,500 |
| 9 → 10 | 3,200 |

## Earning XP

- **Kills.** Any recruited companion near a creature's death (within ~20 m) earns XP — it doesn't have to land the killing blow. XP scales with the **biome** and the **toughness** of what died: a tougher creature in a more dangerous biome is worth much more. A Meadows Greyling is worth a little; an Ashlands warrior is worth a lot.
- **Player kills** (on PvP servers) grant a flat amount.
- **Duel wins** grant a bonus — see [Dvergr Duels](Dvergr-Duels).

Rough per-kill scale, from weakest to toughest common creature in each biome: Meadows ~5 → Black Forest ~12 → Swamp ~22 → Mountain ~35 → Plains ~50 → Mistlands ~75 → Ashlands ~100.

## What leveling does

- **Stronger.** Each level scales the companion's **health and damage** — including a mage's spell damage.
- **Caste flavor.** On top of that, front-line castes (Rogue, Fire Mage) gain **move speed** as they level, and back-line castes (Ice Mage, Support Mage) gain **max health** — up to a meaningful bonus at level 10.

## Seeing a companion's level

- A gold **★N badge** on the floating name above the companion shows its level at a glance.
- Hovering your own companion shows the detailed readout: `Companion · Lv 7 (42% to next)`, or `Lv 10 (max)` once capped.

Levels are never lost — duels are non-lethal and only ever add XP, never remove it.
